WHO ARE WE

we are a newly started company which not just in the business of boat safaris, we're in the business of crafting memories that will last a lifetime. Our journey began with a profound love for the magnificent Brahmaputra River, its diverse ecosystem, and the desire to share this natural wonder with adventure-seeking souls like you.

Our Mission

Our mission at wiloo is to inspire the adventurous spirit within every customer. We aim to connect your dreams of exploring the untamed beauty of the Brahmaputra River with reality, providing affordable, unforgettable boat safaris that leave a lasting impact.

Our Story

We invite you to embark on an extraordinary journey with us. Whether you're a wildlife enthusiast, a photography buff, or simply seeking an escape from the ordinary, Wiloo promises an adventure that will leave you with stories to tell and memories to cherish.
